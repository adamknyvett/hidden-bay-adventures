Scenic Sea Tour

Hidden Coast Explorer

🕑 1 hour ⚓ Departs Waihi Beach 👥 Up to 6 guests

An hour-long coastal voyage tracing the edge of the Coromandel and Bay of Plenty boundary. Sea caves and rock arches carved by the ocean, hidden waterfalls only reachable by sea, gold-era mine workings cut into cliffsides, nesting seabirds on remote rock stacks and the chance of incidental marine wildlife along the way. Bookings open September 2026.

  • Sea caves and rock arches along the coastline
  • Coastal waterfalls accessible only by sea
  • Historic gold mine workings on the cliffs
  • Gannet, shearwater & seabird colonies
  • Incidental marine wildlife — sharks, rays, dolphins & more
  • All ages welcome — life jackets supplied
